FututEd AI

Code the Classroom of Tomorrow

This hackathon challenges innovators to develop AI-driven educational solutions that can improve learning outcomes, accessibility, and efficiency for students and educators. Participants will leverage cutting-edge AI tools to create impactful learning experiences.

Where and When

UOC, Computer Science Department
2-3 October 2025

The Computer Science Department at the University of Crete will host the FuturEd AI event, providing an excellent collaborative workspace where teams can participate in workshops and receive mentoring from industry experts.

This exciting event, packed with inspirational coding, mentoring sessions, networking, and plenty of food, drinks, and surprises, will begin on Thursday, October 2nd, 2025 at 10:00 AM and conclude on Friday, October 3rd, 2025 at 8:00 PM.
