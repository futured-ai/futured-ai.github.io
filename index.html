Code the Future of Learning

Participants will utilize advanced AI techniques to solve real educational problems, shaping the future of learning for millions.

Challenge

Develop innovative, AI-driven solutions that revolutionize education by creating personalized, accessible, and engaging learning experiences. Participants are tasked with leveraging artificial intelligence to address real educational problems, from personalized learning companions and real-time knowledge gap identification to adaptive visualization and improved accessibility—ultimately shaping a future where education is more effective and equitable for all.

Organizers

The event is co-organized by Epignosis and the Computer Science Department of the University of Crete.

Epignosis

Epignosis, the company behind TalentLMS, is a global leader in learning technologies committed to democratizing online training. With a focus on user-friendly and effective solutions, they empower organizations of all sizes to easily create and deliver engaging learning experiences.

Computer Science Department, University of Crete

The Computer Science Department of the University of Crete offers an undergraduate and postgraduate program of high quality and conducts research with tangible and important results in areas of significance for the industry and society. The educational and research activities of the Department cover a wide scope of basic and applied areas in Computer Science.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event, typically lasting a few days, where people like programmers, designers, and other professionals collaborate intensively to solve problems or create new projects. These events, a blend of "hack" and "marathon," foster rapid innovation and teamwork, often resulting in working prototypes or solutions presented to judges.

What is an AI hackathon?

An AI hackathon is an intensive, collaborative event where programmers, data scientists, and AI enthusiasts gather to develop innovative AI-powered solutions within a set timeframe.

Is registration to FuturEd AI free?

Yes, registration to FuturEd AI is completely free. Participation is limited to selected teams.

Who can participate in FuturEd AI?

Anyone with an interest in technology, problem-solving, and collaboration can participate, as long as they form a team of 2 to 3 people.

What should I bring to the FuturEd AI?

Bring your laptop, charger, any preferred peripherals, and your teammates. Most importantly, bring an open mind and a collaborative spirit!

Do I need to have prior experience?

Not at all! FuturEd AI is open to all skill levels. You'll find mentors and teammates ready to support you throughout the event.

Can I bring a pre-existing project?

No. All projects must be started from scratch during the event to ensure fairness and creativity.

What is the selection process?

Applicants submit their skills and ideas. A committee will select the most promising teams based on creativity, technical skills, and relevance to the theme.

What is the judging rubric?

Please refer to the “Judging Procedure” section for complete details.

Will there be mentors?

Yes! Experienced mentors will guide you during the event, answer questions, and help shape your ideas.

How can I stay updated?

Follow our official website and social media channels for all announcements and updates.

How do I collect my prize?

Prizes are distributed via gift cards to a tech store of your choice, sent by email. Prize sharing within the team is up to you!

When will the prizes be given?

Winners will receive their gift cards no later than October 31, 2025.
