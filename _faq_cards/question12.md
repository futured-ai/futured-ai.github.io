title: How do I collect my prize?
answer: >
  Congratulations! If your team wins an award category, your prize will be awarded as a gift card (not cash), with a value of €2,000 per category. The gift cards will be emailed to each winning team. The event organizers will reach out to guide you through the process. How you choose to divide the prize among your team is entirely up to you.
